Biography

Sebastian Berndt is a postdoc in the group of Prof. Dr. Klaus Jansen at Kiel University. His research interests revolve around intractable problems, which he tries to solve with approaches such as approximation algorithms or fixed-parameter-tractability. On the other hand, he applies these problems to cryptography and steganography to enable secure communication.
