Biography

Sebastian Berndt is a postdoc in the group of Prof. Dr. Klaus Jansen at the University of Kiel. His research interest include algorithmic approaches to intractable (i.e. $\mathcal{NP}$-hard) problems such as approximation algorithms or fixed-parameter-tractability, online algorithms and foundations of cryptography and steganography.
